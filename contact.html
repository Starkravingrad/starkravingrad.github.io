Bio

Residing in Leavenworth, WA. Garrett Criss forges his own unique craft and style to this art. Using sounds of nature and synthesized frequencies. smooth jazz inspired low-end occupies his sets. Noting artists such as Dizzy Gillespie and Django Reinhardt as some of his influences the chord progressioning of his songs is vaguely reminiscient of a 40's speakeasy sit in band. Working as a classically trained audio-engineer in his freetime he's been recently sampling orchestral and opera styles into his productions for added atmosphere. This is a project for experimentation leaving no genre untouched.
