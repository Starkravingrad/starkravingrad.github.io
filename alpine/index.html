When do we raft?

White water rafting trips are generally available 7 days a week during the peak river rafting months in Washington State of May, June, July, August. Our regular season starts at the end of April and runs into October. We run Fall Colors trips on the Upper Skagit in October and November and our Skagit River Bald Eagle watching trips run December through February.

Whats Included?

All whitewater rafting trips include neoprene booties, neoprene wetsuit, personal flotation device, splash jacket, a helmet when required, shuttle vehicles for transportation, and a trained and experienced river guide. For more info on what you should bring/wear, see the "What should I wear?"
